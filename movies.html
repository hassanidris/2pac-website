Poetic Justice (1993)

Role: Lucky

Genres: Drama . Romance

Director: John Singleton

Writer: John Singleton

Stars: Janet Jackson . Tupac Shakur . Regina King

Storyline: After witnessing her boyfriend’s murder, Justice abandons college to become a hairdresser in South Central Los Angeles. She copes with her depression through poetry. Juice (1992)

Role: Roland Bishop

Genres: Action . Crime . Drama . Thriller

Director: Ernest R. Dickerson

Writer: Ernest R. Dickerson . Gerard Brown

Stars: Omar Epps . Tupac Shakur . Jermaine Hopkins

Storyline: 4 Harlem teens, Q, Bishop, Raheem and Steel, are out skipping school one day when they find out an old friend was killed in a shootout at a bar. After this, Bishop tells his friends that they have no respect, or juice. To get some, they rob a corner grocery store, but things take an unexpected turn. Only the four friends know what happened, but one of them is out for himself.
