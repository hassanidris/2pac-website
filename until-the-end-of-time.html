Until the End of Time

Release date: 2001

Label: Amaru Entertainment

Until the End of Time is the seventh studio album and third posthumous album by American rapper 2Pac. It follows his previous posthumous albums R U Still Down? and Still I Rise. The album consists of material recorded while the rapper was on Death Row Records from 1995 to 1996.
